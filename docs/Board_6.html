A Reverse bid (e.g., 1C then 2H) is an artificial forcing bid requiring specific minimum strength (usually 16+ HCP) and shape (usually a 5-card opening suit followed by a 4-card suit higher ranking than the first bid suit). If you do not have the required 16+ HCP, you must find a more conservative rebid, such as simply rebidding your original suit at the 2-level (2C), or bidding 1NT if balanced. West's 2H bid with only 14 HCP and a 2-card Heart suit was misleading, risking an unwarranted Game attempt by partner.
